What is Campus Connect ?

According to the founders Aryan and Kartik , Campus Connect is a centralised platform for all the information regarding a college. Initially built to sell the merchandise for their branch, Campus Connect evolved from just an idea to a full-blown project. The aim of CampusConnect is to let students have access to all the information about their college at one place. They aim to create a free, open and accessible space where anyone can contribute and grow. Let's build and grow in this space, together.

What we aim to achieve ?

Enhancing campus space

Build a space of passionate minds who want to be contribute and be part of the changes occuring around them.

Networking

Connecting with different bright minds and help them learn and grow. And you surely can contribute to our cause too :)

Strengthen the community

Learning and teaching simultaneously and working towards strengthening the community.

So the alternative way through which students can meet new people and expand their network without infringing upon their studies is through joining the college clubs and participating in various events.
